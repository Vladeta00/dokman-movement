About me

Who am I?

My name is Stefan and I'm from Serbia. Ever since I was a teenager I was interested in calisthenics and streetworkout. I have 8+ years of experience. I've been travelling across Serbia and I have been seeking to learn more and more about both my body and mind. I have also taken interests in Eastern philosophies of body and mind and I'm happy that I can share my experiences and knowledge with my students.

My skills and knowledge

From the humble beginnings of doing pull ups, planche, and front-lever I have learned so much more. My main focus now is on the movement of the body. On controlling the balance and flexibility of muscles and joints. I'm also studying the philosophy behind the connection of our mind and our muscles. My current repertoire of skills are calisthenics, gymnastics, animal movement, and yoga.
